story

Hi, Hi I'm Anila!
I am a girl who grew up in the country, Since I was a child, I hoped to bring a better life to my family through education, so I was diligent in learning.


After graduating from high school, I went to Taipei to study at Shilin High School of Commerce, Department of Data Processing, While studying and working part-time, I also actively participated in the Concert Band. During my Concert Band period, I participated in many national music flag competitions, performances in on-campus and off-campus activities, and the opening of corporate events.


Later, the university was admitted to the first choice - Taipei University of Technology, Department of Information and Financial Management. During college, in addition to studying hard, I also participated in the student government and program-related communities to improve my soft and hard power.


In addition, I am also engaged in different internships and jobs, while exploring the direction of my future career. while learning Japanese. After college, I joined a digital financial insurance company and worked as a data analyst. In 2022, I decided to switch careers as a frontend engineer.


In order to go to work in Japan, I concentrate on improving my Japanese and programming skills, continue to accumulate front-end technology and experience, and hope to make better products. At the same time, I will continue to operate my own blog, sorting out the knowledge and technology I have learned and sharing it with others. many more people.


I look forward to making more contributions on this road, and even organize reading clubs or exchange meetings to gather interested people to learn front-end technologies or other fields of knowledge. Give back to the people who have been taking care of me, give back to this society to help more people!
