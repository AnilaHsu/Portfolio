story

Hi, I'm Anila!
I grew up in a tea village with limited learning and living resources. My dream since childhood was to bring a better life to my family and to have more ability to give back to the society and help more people, so I kept learning and growing through various challenges.


After graduating from secondary school, I went to Taipei to study data processing at Shihlin College of Commerce. While studying and working part-time, I actively participated in the wind band. During my time in the band, I participated in many national wind music competitions, as well as playing at events on and off campus.


Later, the university was admitted to the first choice - Taipei University of Technology, Department of Information and Financial Management. During my college years, besides studying hard, I served as an officer in the student union for three years. I was also actively involved in national information communities and served as an officer of the programming research club at school, learning programming and Japanese on my own. In addition, I also worked in different internships and jobs to explore my future career direction.


After graduating from university, I joined a digital finance and insurance company as a data analyst. In 2002, I decided to turn a page in my life and develop my career as a frontend engineer. I also joined Worklife in Japan as a frontend engineer.


In order to make better products while enjoying development, I am constantly accumulating my skills and experience, and I also hope to share the knowledge and techniques I have learned along the way to more and more people. I look forward to making more contributions along the way, and even organizing book clubs or exchanges to gather people interested in learning technology and knowledge. Help more people through my little power!
